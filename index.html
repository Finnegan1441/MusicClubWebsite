Week 5

Joey Bada$$- 1999

Genre: Hip Hop

Chosen by Patrick

Summary

1999 is the debut mixtape of Joey Bada$$ from Pro Era. The mixtape features production from MF DOOM, J Dilla, Lord Finesse, and many others. This mixtape was well recieved by music critics around the country and launched Joey Bada$$ and Pro Era into the limelight. The name 1999 refers to the end of the "Golden Age" of hip-hop, which Joey Bada$$ tried to replicate the sound of in this mixtape.

Week 4

J Dilla - Donuts

Genre: Hip Hop

Suggested by Tyler

Summary

Donuts is the last and most well known album from J Dilla. Dilla made this album while hospitalized from TTP, which he would eventually die from. Despite being stuck in the hospital and having trouble doing basic tasks, Dilla made this album with his records and a sampler that his friends would bring him. This album has been one of the most influential hip hop albums since it's time of release. Every song on this album is under 2 minutes, so dont let the long tracklist discourage. Also this is an album that I would really encourage you to listen to all at once.

Week 3

Beach House - Teen Dream

Genre: Indie Rock, Dream Pop

Suggested by Ryan

Summary

Teen Dream is the third studio album by American dream pop duo Beach House, released in January 2010. Produced by Chris Coady, Teen Dream is Beach House's Sub Pop debut. The album was well received by the music press, who hailed it as one of the best albums of 2010.

Week 2

Built to Spill - Perfect from Now On

Genre: Indie Rock

Chosen by Marco

Summary

Perfect from Now On is Built to Spill’s third full length album which was released in 1997. It was recorded three different times in the studio and unlike the previous Built to Spill albums, Perfect from Now On features much longer songs. This album also does not follow the traditional verse-chorus-verse format and is considered to have had a large influence on indie rock following its release.

Tame Impala - Currents

Genre: Psychadelic Rock

Chosen by Patrick

Summary

Currents is the third studio album by Australian psychedelic rock act Tame Impala, to be released on 17 July 2015 on Interscope Records. Like the group's previous two albums, Currents was written, recorded, performed, and produced by primary member Kevin Parker. For the first time, Parker also mixed the music.
